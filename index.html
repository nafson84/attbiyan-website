About Us

AT-TIBYAN INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY was established at the year 2014 in Bauchi State, Nigeria. AT-TIBYAN is duly registered with Cooperate Affair Commission(CAC) in Nigeria with Registration Number BA4126.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

We believe in given back to the society, whereby we give free education to some orphans in our host community.

GOVERNING BOARD

The school governing board span around people in active service in clinical practice, Academics and technical expertise in notable higher institutions of learning in Nigeria. Our steering team comprises of people with decade(s)experience in running and supervising private institutions and Innovative businesses

STAFF STRENGTH AND CAPACITY BUILDING

Our staffs are categorized into academics and non academics. Currently with over 70 staff in total. To Date we were able to retrain our staffs confidence and engage them in updates courses and workshops similarly, ensuring uncompromising welfare.

LOCAL COLLABORATION

We have a a network of almost 5 schools within the state, that we collaborate interms of skill transfer, curriculum sharing and students motivational activities.

TECHNOLOGICAL CAPACITY

Our Institution is among the first to deploy a fully digitize system of Operation among it peers. We currently operate on a fully digital management system from academics to financial activities. With a transparent system.
